About Me

I received my B.A. in Anthropology with a minor in Psychology from Texas Tech University. After graduation, I went on to pursue an alternative teaching certificate in EC-12 which led me to teach middle school art for 2 years. After which, I started working on my own artwork, and worked as a freelance graphic designer making logos and business cards.

All the vegetables in my art pieces were posed and manipulated by me. For these pieces, I had an ongoing experiment where I would let vegetables wilt, rot, or hold dissections to see how they re-grew themselves or to alter their apearance. While one of my produce paintings resembles natural geography, my aim was to alter the fruit or vegetable in a way that mimicked the human anatomy.
